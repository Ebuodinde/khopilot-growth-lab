# Growth-Sprint Gameplan

Read this when the task is a follower-growth sprint (grow from a small base to a target), not a single video. It is the operating procedure — what to attend to. Topic-free; the tenant supplies the subject, lanes, and numbers.

## Frame: the account is a hook laboratory

The job is to **find repeatable hold+send formats**, not to "post nice videos." Treat the run as a series of experiments whose real output is *data on what works for this audience*. A target follower count is a **hypothesis and an unlock condition, never a promise** (see `calibration.md`).

## The funnel you are actually optimizing

Followers come from **non-follower reach → profile visit → follow.** Likes are vanity. Every piece must: clear **HOLD** (survive the 3s gate) → earn **SEND** (break into non-follower reach) → end on a **payoff/identity that gives a follow reason.** Track the funnel, not likes.

## Prerequisites before any posting (or reach is wasted)

- **Profile converts in ~2 seconds:** a visitor must instantly get "what this is / who it's for." Consistent identity, a pinned set, a one-line positioning. A breakout piece wastes its reach if the profile does not convert.
- **Data/platform access** set up in advance if metrics are a goal of the sprint.
- **Anti-pollution:** do not solicit likes/follows from off-target contacts — it mis-trains the seed audience and pushes distribution toward the wrong people. Let content re-target. Avoid generic trend/bait that pulls off-niche traffic; niche-consistent content lets the right audience self-select.

## Run every piece as a declared bet (the bet card)

Before posting, declare: **lane/angle · hold-source (question / visual-stake / niche-relevance) · one target metric · the send-trigger** (who would send this to whom).
After posting, record: **3s-skip · completion · sends/reach · profile visits · follows · view-source split (non-follower vs follower).** This is the calibration data.

## Phase shape

- **Discovery:** spread *diverse* bets (different hold-sources × lanes) to learn what holds + sends for this audience. Do not marry the first idea.
- **Exploit:** clone the winning hold-sources (same tension, new angle), kill the losers, sharpen the send-trigger.
- **Scale:** concentrate on the proven format, push the metric trigger hardest.
Each phase ends with a data review that updates the rules before more is produced.

## Decision gates (no blind posting)

- A piece that fails the gate (high 3s-skip) is **not cloned.**
- **Several consecutive gate-failures → stop producing, rethink with the data.** Do not keep dumping volume into a broken hook.
- A **breakout** (non-follower sources dominant + high sends/reach) → **clone immediately.**
- Define a concrete **"growth stalled" trigger** (e.g. N consecutive pieces with no new follows) that forces a data review.

## Diagnose by funnel stage, not by vibe

When reviewing, isolate which stage failed and fix only that:
- **HOLD problem** — high 3s-skip → the opening/hook.
- **SEND problem** — good watch, low sends/reach → no real share-trigger, or identity too weak to recommend.
- **CONVERSION problem** — good reach, low follows → profile/identity does not convert visitors.
Lead every review with the clone/kill decision, then the single next action.

## Calibration is the point

Each measured result becomes a **topic-free inference** in `calibration.md`. Predictions are hypotheses; do not pre-rank winners with confidence (it has been unreliable). Maximize at-bats and learning rate; let real skip/completion/sends rank the bets.
