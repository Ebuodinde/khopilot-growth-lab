# Pre-launch Growth System

Read this when planning the account, a week of content, or a content experiment.

## Goal

Khopilot is not live yet. Optimize for followers, excitement, profile visits, saves, comments, sends, Story replies, and beta demand. Do not optimize for installs.

## Position

Khopilot should feel like the account mapping Turkey's viraj culture before it feels like an app ad. People follow for the content first; the product becomes the natural result.

## Lanes

- Natural Turkey road: real, local, route/viraj culture. Target follows and sends.
- Thailand absurd: reversal, weird social setup, dry punchline. Target comments and rewatch.
- Product shadow: HUD, curve intelligence, route logic. Target saves and profile visits.
- Community fight: motor vs car, straight road vs viraj. Target comments and Story replies.
- Route lore: saveable local road knowledge. Target saves and shares.

## Weekly Cadence

- Monday: route / viraj / Turkey road.
- Tuesday: joke / trend adaptation.
- Wednesday: product shadow / viraj intelligence.
- Thursday: car vs motorcycle debate.
- Friday: absurd / Thailand-ad style.
- Weekend: Stories, reposts, polls, "which road next?"

## Winner Rules

- Viral winner: sends/reach and comments. Clone the tension.
- Retention winner: average watch, completion, saves. Expand into series.
- Follow winner: follows/reach and profile visits. Pin or make it account identity.
- Paid candidate: product is understood plus profile/save signal. Keep for launch.

Real platform metrics beat TRIBE pre-test output.
