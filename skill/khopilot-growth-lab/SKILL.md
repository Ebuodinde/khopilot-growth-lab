# Khopilot Growth Lab

## Purpose

Use this skill when the user asks for Khopilot pre-launch social content, viral hook concepts, Flow prompts, moodboards, Instagram/Threads/Facebook plans, metric reviews, winner cloning, or content audits. The app may not be live; optimize for followers, excitement, profile visits, saves, comments, shares, and beta demand rather than installs.

## Operating Rule

Do not dump generic idea lists. Produce an experiment-ready content package:

1. Context and lane.
2. Hook and target metric.
3. Three variants: natural, absurd, product-proof.
4. Flow/Palmier/TRIBE production path when relevant.
5. Content quality gates: viral hook, storytelling, dumbify, anti-AI, voice DNA.
6. Caption, Threads post, Story prompt.
7. Metrics to collect.
8. Clone/revise/kill rule.

## Default Strategy

If the app is not live, avoid install CTAs. Use:

- "Bunu viraj seven arkadaşına gönder."
- "Bu yolu bilen var mı?"
- "Motor mu araba mı?"
- "Bunu test listesine alalım mı?"
- "Beta listesine girmek isteyen DM'den 'viraj' yazsın."

Keep tone Turkish-first, natural, non-generic, and realistic. The humor may be slightly dirty or absurd, but avoid death/crash fear, illegal speed, radar evasion, and generic AI-ad wording.

## Tool Logic

- Flow: generate video/image variants; use Lite/Fast first, Quality only for winners.
- Palmier: organize rough cuts, timeline variants, exports.
- TRIBE v2: compare same-brief variants before publishing; never treat it as a guaranteed virality oracle.
- Instagram/Reels: final organic test surface.
- Threads: topic discussion and community signal, not copied captions.
- Meta Ads: only after organic winners and app/event readiness.
- Content quality gates: run viral-hooks, storytelling, dumbify, anti-AI, and voice-DNA checks before shipping scripts or captions.

## Output Style

For concept work, use compact tables plus one detailed first pick. For execution work, produce copy-paste-ready prompts and captions. For metric reviews, lead with winner/loser decisions and next actions.
